Our Story

Crafted with Love in Ghatkopar

Shree Krishna Hot & Fresh Chips began as a small family-run shop in the heart of Ghatkopar East, Mumbai — guided by the belief that honest snacks need no shortcuts.

From signature Dhebra Khakhras to crispy banana chips, every product is made in small batches. No artificial colors. No preservatives. Just real ingredients and real love.
